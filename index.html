About THDC INSTITUTE OF HYDROPOWER ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLGY

THDC Institute of Hydropower Engineering and Technology is a newly established (in Aug 2011) first constituent institute of Uttarakhand Technical University with the aim of becoming a world class comprehensive institute with mission of high-quality undergraduate programs and high caliber research development in cutting-edge areas. The institute is situated at B. Puram, Tehri, approximately 150 km from capital city – Dehradun on beautiful site of THDC project.
